The Last Paddle, Laddle

  • High Concept: A turn based based grid strategy game where you have to collect all the booty while avoiding the ghosts
  • Player Experience Goals: Deep intellectual thought for the strategy and competetion for the highest score
  • Genre: Turn Based Strategy
  • Game play: You must move the Paddle around the arena with WASD to collect the chests in the area. You get to move one space per turn and then the ghosts take a turn. This continues until you have collected all the chests in the arena
  • Prototype goal: Turn based movement
  • Features: Turn system where your movement is more limited than in most games. Risk and Reward in regards to the ghosts random movements
  • Setting/Premise: Set in the ancient home of the Paddle Clan
  • Story: Laddle is the last of the Paddle clan. His final mission? Reclaim the ancient home of the Paddle Clan from the Ghosts
  • Target Audience: Ages 10-18
  • Play time: about 10 minutes
  • Strategies: Moving around such that you won't get surrounded, and realizing that you don't have to kill the ghosts to get to the stairs
  • Prototype Assets: Source Code: Custom authored by Oscar Quinteros Art Assets: Custom made by Oscar Quinteros Audio Assets: Music -- Possess by William Hellfire -- http://freemusicarchive.org/music/William_Hellfire/Upsidedown_Cross_Soundtrack_1758/Poses_-_William_Hellfire chests -- http://opengameart.org/content/key-pickup hits -- http://www.bfxr.net/ scream -- http://opengameart.org/content/horror-scream1
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Inspired by Fire Emblem, XCOM. No Phaser example game used
  • Instructions: Clcik to select a tile, ENTER to move to it. Attack by clicking on a ghost when you're next to it. You can only attack after moving and Ghosts can't attack and move on the same turn. Ghosts inflict 10 damage to you and you inflict 10 damage to them. Killing a ghost will heal you for 20 health. Head towards the staircase to move down to the next level.
