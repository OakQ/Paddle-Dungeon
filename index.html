The Last Paddle, Laddle

  • High Concept: A turn based based grid strategy game where you have to collect all the booty while avoiding the ghosts
  • Player Experience Goals: Deep intellectual thought for the strategy and competetion for the highest score
  • Genre: Turn Based Strategy
  • Game play: You must move the Paddle around the arena with WASD to collect the chests in the area. You get to move one space per turn and then the ghosts take a turn. This continues until you have collected all the chests in the arena
  • Prototype goal: Turn based movement
  • Features: Turn system where your movement is more limited than in most games. Risk and Reward in regards to the ghosts random movements
  • Setting/Premise: Set in the ancient home of the Paddle Clan
  • Story: Seedle is the last of the Sunflower clan. His final mission? Reclaim the ancient home of the Sunflower Clan from the Hamsters
  • Target Audience: Ages 10-18
  • Play time: about 10 minutes
  • Strategies: Moving around such that you won't get surrounded, and realizing that you don't have to kill the hamsters to get to the stairs
  • Prototype Assets: Source Code: Oscar Quinteros, Andrew Hyunh Art Assets: Sergio Gutierrez, Oscar Quinteros Audio Assets: Music -- Hamtaro Theme
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Inspired by Fire Emblem, XCOM. No Phaser example game used
  • Instructions: Click to select a tile, ENTER to move to it. Attack by clicking on a Hamster when you're next to it. You can only attack after moving and Hamsters can't attack and move on the same turn. Speedy moves fast but hits weak. Heavy moves slow and hits hard. Boomer hits REALLY hard but knocks himself out. Dr. Hamster heals herself every turn. Meaty has a ton of health. Adn then there's the Boss Hamster, he has a lot of health, moves fast and regens very quickly when his health is low. Your only choice is to run from him. Knocking out any Hamster will heal you for 20 health. Head towards the staircase to move down to the next level.
